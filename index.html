    About Me

    Today marks the first day of the rest of your life. Thats what my bus driver told me when I complained to him about my unlucky life. "I got no hands" I exclaimed, obviously reffering to how bad I am at ball. Dumbfounded, Buzz quickly grabbed my hands and told me with releif, "Don't worry... they are right here." A gentle smile grew on Buzz's face as he thought of all the beauties that we take for granted. His thumbs caressed my knuckles as he gripped my hands more firmly, almost as if he was protecting them from something. "I know that I HAVE hands.. bruhh". Buzz would now wear a scowl until I quietly mutter, "Thank you." Hearing this, Buzz was able to relax at last. He softly let go of my hands and grabbed the steering wheel once more. The point of this story is pretty obvious so I will refrain from "over milking the cow," as my super used to say to me back in Colombus. To the curious few who have read till this point, with some unfounded, yet unremetting hope that you will get to know more about me, I will claim this: I think You know quite a bit about me already, don't you? If you don't find yourself fond of puzzles, then I won't waste another moment of your time: I am student, brother, and friend. I am a son. I am also a tutor, minister, and referee on Saturdays. I am humble, artistic, and a Taurus. Money is meaningless, but I still got it. Thank you. *Bows in front of bathroom mirror while listening to Applause by Lady Gaga*
